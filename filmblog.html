My Favourite Movies

These are my top 9 all time favourite movies across 3 languages: English, Tamil and Malayalam.

#1

Premam is a coming-of-age drama about a man from his teens to his thirties. Premam is the first movie I watched alone. It truly is a life-altering piece of art in my life since 2015.

#2

Inception is a science fiction action film. It is my favourite Chris Nolan film since it has science, drama, thriller and romance. I pride that I understood the movie in one watch.

#3

Inglourious Basterds is a historical fiction war film. I love QT's aesthetic and his action. This film encapsulates all his quirks while giving a satisfying, scary, funny and thrilling experience.

#4

La La Land is a romatic musical film. I am a sucker for romance and, this is the peak. Besides romance, it has comedy and drama and gives a sense of self to the characters.

#5

Super Deluxe is a hyperlink film that focuses on 4 different events affecting each other. This movie makes me think of the purpose of life while still enjoying the little things.

#6

The Nice Guys is a buddy action dark comedy that revolves around two PIs in the 70's in LA. It is such a fun watch with an exhilarating plot.

#7

The Devil's Wear Prada is a comedy-drama film that revolves around a new graduate trying to find herself and passion while working for a not-so-nice boss. It is a must-watch for everyone.

#8

Zodiac is a mystery-thriller film based on the real-life investigation of the Zodiac killer. David Fincher is one of my favourites and this is one of his best (I love thrillers).

#9

Maanagaram is a hyperlink action-thriller film that happens in one night involving multiple characters, all meeting at the same point. It is a fun yet thrilling watch.
