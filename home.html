Who Am I

Student

I am a third-year student at the University of Toronto, Mississauga (UTM), majoring in Computer Science and CCIT. I am also the co-president of a CS-oriented academic club called the UTMSAM. Besides spending time with my friends, I like to learn about software design, UX/UI design, and media production.

Intern

Being interested in tech, I have cumulatively done 12 months of internship in two different companies. I started as a technical analyst at Bell Canada, then a junior developer at CGI and this summer as a software developer at Bell Canada. All my internships have helped me in exploring my interests.

Visual Artist

Ever since I knew what a crayon was, I have always been interested in visual arts. I experiment with pastels, watercolours, acrylics and pencil shading techniques. Although my strong suit is painting, I have also started experimenting with digital art using apps like sketchbook on my Ipad.

Cyclist

I always prefer to bike everywhere I can and find as many new & peaceful places as I can. Most of my childhood memories are filled with biking on busy roads and places way farther than my house without my parents' knowledge. This summer I completed 500km in a month for a charity.

Filmbuff

I watched my first movie when I was 2. So, movies and I are inseparable. I prefer watching movies over reading and that's how I try to learn about new cultures, languages and concepts. I primarily watch movies in four languages. My favourite genres are coming-of-age dramas and psychological thrillers.
